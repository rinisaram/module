Introduction to Email and Online Communication

Objectives

  • Understand what email is and its importance.
  • Learn how to create an email account.
  • Learn how to send and receive emails.
  • Learn basic online communication etiquette.

What is Email?

Email (electronic mail) is a method of sending and receiving messages over the internet. It is one of the most common forms of online communication, allowing people to send text, images, and files.

How to Create an Email Account

Here’s how to create an email account using Gmail:

  • Go to www.gmail.com.
  • Click 'Create account.'
  • Enter your name, choose a username, and set a password.
  • Follow the steps to verify your account with your mobile number and email.

How to Send and Receive Emails

To send an email, follow these steps:

  • Log into your email account (e.g., Gmail).
  • Click on 'Compose' or 'New Message.'
  • Enter the recipient’s email address, subject, and the message content.
  • Click 'Send' to send the email.

Attaching Files and Images to an Email

To attach files or images:

  • Look for a paperclip icon while composing an email.
  • Click on it, choose the file or image from your device, and it will be attached.
  • Click 'Send' after attaching the file.

Basic Online Communication Etiquette

Here are some tips for effective online communication:

  • Use proper greetings (e.g., 'Dear,' 'Hello').
  • Keep your message short and to the point.
  • Check for spelling and grammar errors before sending.
